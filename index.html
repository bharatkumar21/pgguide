MA Entrance Exams

Popular Exams:

  • Central Universities Entrance Test (CUET-PG): For admission to MA programs in Central Universities and other participating institutions.
  • Jawaharlal Nehru University Entrance Exam (JNUEE): MA programs at JNU.
  • Banaras Hindu University Postgraduate Entrance Test (BHU PET): Admission to MA at BHU.
  • Delhi University Entrance Test (DUET): For MA and other postgraduate courses at DU.

Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree in relevant discipline. Minimum percentage varies by university.

Syllabus: Subject knowledge, general aptitude, social sciences, literature (based on course).

MCA Entrance Exams

Major Exams:

  • NIT MCA Common Entrance Test (NIMCET): Entry to NITs for MCA programs.
  • MAH MCA CET: For MCA admissions in Maharashtra universities.
  • CUET-PG: Participating Central Universities.
  • WB JECA: MCA admissions in West Bengal.

Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree with Mathematics as a subject.

Syllabus: Mathematics, Logical Reasoning, Computer Awareness, General English.

MBA Entrance Exams

Top Exams:

  • Common Admission Test (CAT): For IIMs and other B-Schools.
  • Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT): XLRI and other management colleges.
  • Management Aptitude Test (MAT): Accepting B-Schools across India.
  • Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT): For global MBA programs.
  • Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP): For Symbiosis institutes.
  • MAH MBA CET: MBA programs in Maharashtra.

Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50% marks (varies).

Syllabus: Quantitative Aptitude, Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, General Awareness.
